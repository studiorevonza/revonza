## Content Calendar Structure

### Monthly Publishing Schedule:
- Week 1: Educational/How-to post
- Week 2: Case study or success story
- Week 3: Industry news/trend analysis
- Week 4: Comparison/review post

### Content Format Mix:
- 60% Educational articles (800-1500 words)
- 20% Case studies/testimonials
- 10% Industry news and trends
- 10% Tool reviews/comparisons

## SEO Content Optimization Guidelines

### Title Structure:
Primary Keyword + Secondary Keyword + Brand/Location + Value Proposition

Examples:
- "Web Development for Startups: Complete Guide to Building Your MVP | Revonza Studio"
- "SEO Services for Small Business: 12 Tactics That Actually Work in 2026"
- "Affordable Branding Agency India: How to Get Professional Design on a Budget"

### Meta Description Template:
Clear value proposition + specific service + trust signal + CTA

Examples:
"Expert web development and SEO services for startups and small businesses. Get affordable branding solutions with proven results. Free consultation available."

### Content Structure:
1. **Hook** (100-150 words) - Address pain point
2. **Introduction** (150-200 words) - Establish expertise
3. **Main Content** (800-1200 words) - Comprehensive information
4. **FAQ Section** (200-300 words) - Answer common questions
5. **Call-to-Action** (100-150 words) - Direct to service

## Technical SEO for Blog Posts

### URL Structure:
/blog/[keyword-focused-slug]

Examples:
- /blog/web-development-cost-india-2026
- /blog/seo-checklist-small-business
- /blog/ai-website-development-trends

### Image Optimization:
- File names: [keyword]-[descriptive]-[brand].jpg
- Alt text: Descriptive with target keyword naturally included
- Compression: WebP format, under 100KB when possible

### Schema Markup:
- Article schema for blog posts
- FAQ schema for question-answer sections
- Breadcrumb schema for navigation
